YouTube Rules

CORE COMMUNITY RULES

Respect the Grind

Respect the creator, moderators, and fellow viewers. Hate speech, discrimination, harassment, or personal attacks will result in immediate moderation action.

No Toxic Energy

Friendly banter is allowed. Targeted harassment, bullying, or repeated negativity is not. Keep the vibes competitive — not destructive.

No NSFW Content

Inappropriate language, sexual comments, or explicit content in comments or usernames will not be tolerated.

No Impersonation

Pretending to be the creator, staff, or other members is strictly prohibited.

No Fake Giveaways / Scams

Posting scam links, crypto scams, fake giveaways, or suspicious links will result in instant permanent ban.

💬 COMMENT SECTION RULES

No Spam Flooding

Avoid repetitive messages, emoji spam, ALL CAPS spam, copy-paste chains, or irrelevant comments.

No Self Promotion

Do not advertise your YouTube channel, Discord server, Instagram, or any platform unless officially allowed.

Stay On Topic

Keep discussions related to the video. Off-topic arguments or drama will be removed.

No Sensitive Debates

Religion, politics, global conflicts, or controversial debates are not allowed in comments.

No Spoilers (If Applicable)

If the content involves story-based games, avoid posting spoilers without warning.

🔥 LIVE STREAM RULEBOOK

Respect Live Flow

Do not interrupt the stream with repeated demands, spam, or unrelated messages.

No Backseat Gaming (Unless Asked)

Suggestions are welcome. Ordering, insulting, or repeatedly correcting gameplay is not.

No Chat Wars

Do not start arguments or provoke others in live chat.

No Stream Disruption

Sound spam, trolling behavior, fake emergency messages, or attempts to derail the stream will result in timeout or ban.

Respect Personal Boundaries

Avoid asking private questions about personal life, income, relationships, or location.

Clip Responsibly

If clipping streams, do not edit content to spread misinformation or create drama.

Discord Rules

💎 GENERAL RULES

Respect All Players

Every member here is part of the squad. Trash talk is fine in fun limits, but harassment, discrimination, or targeting someone personally is not allowed. Keep it competitive, not toxic.

No NSFW Content

This is a gaming community, keep it clean. NSFW content, inappropriate PFPS, usernames, nicknames, or statuses are strictly prohibited.

Zero Toxicity Policy

Racism, slurs, threats, extreme abusive language, or repeated toxic behavior will result in warnings, mutes, or bans. Skill issue? Improve. Don’t insult.

💬 CHAT RULES

No Spam Attacks

No message flooding, excessive CAPS, emoji spam, lyric spam, copy-paste walls, link spam, GIF floods, or reaction spam. Keep chat readable like a clean HUD.

Keep It Clean

No NSFW jokes, images, or inappropriate language in any channel.

No Public Drama

Got beef? Take it to DMs. Server chat is not a battlefield for personal fights.

No Sensitive Wars

Religion, politics, or international conflict debates are disabled here. This server is for gaming, not global arguments.

No Unauthorized Promotion

No advertising other servers, YouTube channels, or outside platforms unless you have the required role and use the designated promo channel.

🎙 VOICE CHANNEL RULES

No Voice Trolling

Don’t annoy, interrupt, or deliberately disturb others in VC.

No Earrape / Sound Abuse

Loud screaming, mic distortion, inappropriate songs, or soundboards used to disturb others will result in instant mute.

Respect in VC

Same respect rules apply in voice chat. Competitive energy is fine, toxicity is not.

ASRP – Aafat Syndicate Roleplay

Respect All Players (OOC & IC)

Treat everyone with respect both In Character (IC) and Out of Character (OOC).

Harassment, racism, discrimination, threats, or hate speech will not be tolerated under any circumstances.

Toxic behavior = Strict action.

No Hacking or Unfair Modifications

The following are strictly prohibited:

• Hack clients

• X-ray

• Fly / Speed hacks

• Auto-clickers / Macros

• Freecam

• Radar / Illegal minimaps

• Any unfair PvP modification

ASRP is skill + roleplay based.

Cheating = Permanent Ban.

No Powergaming / Metagaming

💎

Powergaming – Forcing unrealistic actions or outcomes on other players without giving them a fair chance to respond.

Metagaming – Using OOC information for IC advantage.

Roleplay must remain realistic and immersive at all times.

No Random Killing (RDM) / Random Raiding

💎

Killing or attacking players without valid RP reason is not allowed.

Every conflict must have proper roleplay buildup.

No random robberies without RP scenario.

No Bug Exploiting

💎

Exploiting glitches, duplication bugs, or server errors for advantage is strictly forbidden.

Report all bugs immediately.

Abuse = Severe punishment.

No Impersonation

Do not pretend to be:

• Staff

• Admin

• Content Creator

• Syndicate Leader

• Another Player

Identity fraud = Ban.

Maintain RP Standards

💎

• Stay in character during RP situations

• Do not break immersion

• Use realistic names & skins

• Avoid troll builds or meme behavior

This is Aafat Syndicate Roleplay, not casual survival.

No IRL Trading

Real money trading of:

• In-game money

• Syndicate ranks

• Items

• Properties

Is strictly prohibited.

No Lag Machines / Server Abuse

💎

Lag machines, excessive entity stacking, redstone abuse, or chunk overload builds will be removed.

Repeated offense = Removal from server.

Chat Language Policy

Keep communication clear for all players.

Respect Staff & RP Admins

💎

Staff decisions are final.

Arguing publicly or harassing staff will result in punishment.

If you disagree — use the proper appeal system.
